About

CRE Consulting, LLC (CREC) is a woman-owned small business with a diverse staff equipped to handle nearly any real estate need. We strive to accurately, promptly and expertly handle any request.

We appreciate the opportunity to advise how we can assist you in your next opportunity.

Summer Roots, M.A.

Principal / President

Summer has been with CREC since 2015 and was hired to grow the Residential Valuation Division. After completing all of her Appraisal Trainee curriculum, she proceeded to expand the residential division which resulted in the division representing approximately half of all revenues.

In 2021, Summer acquired the company with specific desire in expanding the Commercial and Governmental sectors including that of the Right of Way division. Summer leads all vision for the firm and daily management and financial responsibility of the organization.

Jacob Volbers

Right of Way Appraiser

Jacob, a Certified Residential Appraiser with more than 4 years of experience, has been with CREC since 2023. Jacob adeptly evaluates properties across Indianapolis, Carmel, Fishers, Columbus, Greenwood, and beyond.

His meticulous analyses and market insights enhance valuation accuracy. Jacob's commitment to excellence underscores his significant contributions to informed real estate valuation.
